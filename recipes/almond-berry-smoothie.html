Almond Berry Smoothie

Desription

Almond milk and almond butter are the star ingredients in this berry smoothie for a nutritious, on-the-go meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • water as needed

Steps

  1. Combine blueberries, banana, almond milk and almond butter in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Add water to thin out smoothie
