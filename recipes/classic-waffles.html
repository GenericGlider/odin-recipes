Classic Waffles

Description

A simple recipe for tasty homemade waffles if premade waffles just don cut it out for you!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar
  2. Beat the egg, stir in milk, butter and vanilla
  3. Combine the two mixtures
  4. Pour the batter into waffle iron and cook until golden and crisp
